About Cats

Fascinating Feline Facts

Cats are remarkable animals with a variety of interesting characteristics. They have powerful night vision, allowing them to see at light levels six times lower than those required for human vision. Cats also have a strong sense of smell, estimated to be 14 times greater than that of humans!

Did You Know?

  • Cats have a specialized collarbone that allows them to always land on their feet.
  • The average cat can run up to 30 miles per hour.
  • Cats spend approximately 70% of their lives sleeping.
  • A cat’s brain is 90% similar to a human’s brain.
  • The oldest recorded pet cat lived to be 38 years old.

Cat Breeds

  • Abyssinian

    The Abyssinian is known for its playful and active nature. These cats are incredibly curious and love to explore their surroundings.

  • American Bobtail

    The American Bobtail has a distinctive short tail and a wild appearance. They are friendly, intelligent, and enjoy interactive play.

  • American Curl

    The American Curl is easily recognizable by its unique backward-curling ears. They are affectionate, people-oriented, and adapt well to new environments.

  • American Shorthair

    The American Shorthair is a classic, all-American breed known for its robust health, longevity, and friendly personality.

  • American Wirehair

    The American Wirehair has a distinctive wiry coat. These cats are known for their playful and affectionate nature.

  • Balinese

    The Balinese is a long-haired breed that is a natural mutation of the Siamese. They are vocal, social, and love to interact with their human companions.

  • Bengal

    The Bengal has a striking appearance with a leopard-like coat. They are energetic, playful, and highly intelligent.

  • Birman

    The Birman is known for its beautiful, silky coat and striking blue eyes. They are gentle, affectionate, and enjoy human company.

  • Bombay

    The Bombay is often called the "mini-panther" due to its sleek, black coat. They are affectionate, playful, and love to be the center of attention.

  • British Shorthair

    The British Shorthair is a sturdy, well-balanced breed with a short, dense coat. These cats are known for their calm and easygoing nature.

  • Burmese

    The Burmese is known for its muscular build and playful personality. They are affectionate and form strong bonds with their human families.

  • Burmilla

    The Burmilla is a beautiful breed with a shimmering coat. They are playful, affectionate, and enjoy spending time with their owners.

  • Chartreux

    The Chartreux is known for its blue-gray coat and golden eyes. They are quiet, gentle, and make excellent companions.

  • Colorpoint Shorthair

    The Colorpoint Shorthair is a close relative of the Siamese. They are vocal, affectionate, and enjoy human interaction.

  • Cornish Rex

    The Cornish Rex has a distinctive curly coat and an elegant appearance. They are playful, energetic, and love to be the center of attention.

  • Devon Rex

    The Devon Rex is known for its large ears and wavy coat. They are affectionate, mischievous, and enjoy being around people.

  • Egyptian Mau

    The Egyptian Mau is one of the few naturally spotted breeds. They are active, playful, and have a loyal personality.

  • European Burmese

    The European Burmese is a lively and affectionate breed. They are known for their friendly nature and enjoy being part of a family.

  • Exotic Shorthair

    The Exotic Shorthair is a short-haired version of the Persian. They are gentle, affectionate, and easygoing.

  • Havana Brown

    The Havana Brown is known for its rich, chocolate-colored coat. They are intelligent, playful, and form strong bonds with their owners.

  • Himalayan

    The Himalayan is a cross between the Persian and the Siamese. They have a luxurious coat and a calm, gentle personality.

  • Japanese Bobtail

    The Japanese Bobtail is known for its distinctive short, bunny-like tail. They are friendly, active, and bring good luck in Japanese culture.
